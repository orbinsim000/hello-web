### The Man Who Created the Web
Born on June 8, 1955, Tim Berners-Lee is the man who invented the world wide web. Though originally he had a first class degree in physics, he would later go on to sole the problem of sharing information throughout the world. While the internet had already existed, you still couldn't navigate it. Tim Berners-Lee solved that by using hypertext, a way of sharing text.  Using the ability of the Internet and hypertext, he created the first ever web page. Using HTML, HTTP, and a URL you could now navigate the internet.
